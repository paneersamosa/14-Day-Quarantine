What is the 14 Day Quarantine Challenge?

The 14 Day Quarantine Challenge is exactly what it sounds like: it's a 14 day quarantine challenge! For every day of your two week self quarantine, you have a specific challenge to complete, whether it be putting together a COVID-19 care package, making cards for patients, learning more about COVID-19, or keeping yourself healthy! By the end of the challenge, ou will have helped make a difference in your community and in yourself. Hopefully, some of these challenges have helped you find something within yourself! Have fun, and share your challenge results with us on social media @14dayquarantine!
